The story

Drawing insipartaion from the cafe racer culture of 1950s and 60s, the new contentaial GT 650 Twin recaptures the spirit of our contential GT 250. A design icon , the perfect blend of tradition & modernity , with its scuplated tank, eager attitude and a tucked-in riding position , all hallmarks of a traditional cafe racer, is perfect for carving up the back roads or a ton of fun in the city.
