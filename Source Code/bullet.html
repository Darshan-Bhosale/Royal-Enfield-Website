The story

What started in 1932 continues to deliver pure motorcycling experinces across generations of riders. Bullet, the quintessential Royal Enfield, is today the longest running motorcycle in history to be in conyinuous production. A unique, hand-painted fuel tank, th distinct thump and a standing testimony to Royal Enfield's philosophy of simple design and resilient build make Bullet a living legacy on two wheels.

Specification











Engine Type : Single Cylinder, 4-Stroke, Spark Ignition
Engine Displacement (CC) : 499 cc

Transmission : Manual 5-speed
