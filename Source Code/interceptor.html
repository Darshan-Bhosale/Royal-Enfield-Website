The story

Inspire by the 60's interceptor with it's greatest following the american west cost. fitting perfectly in easy going , fun narattive of the time, The new interceptor 650 Twin remagines the sun-drenched California beach lifestyle. This modern classic is the essence of a British roadster with a dash of a California Desert Racer blending timeless style and contemporary performance.

Specification

Fuel Type : Petrol

Tyre Type : Tubeless
